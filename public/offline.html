Setup

  • Create a new game. Or create a "Tour" which can have many games. Perfect for vacations.
  • Choose the number of plates (up to 10) to have a bonus value. Random plates will be marked and will be worth double points
  • Invite your travel mates (or anyone for that matter) to join your game using their gmail account
  • Click Play and start the competition

Playing

  • See a plate? It's a race to click it. Whoever gets it first gets the points
  • Points are based on how far away the plate's state is from your current location.
  • Once a plate is found, no one else can claim it.
  • When done with your trip the person with the most points wins.

After the Game

  • Once the game is over, you can see the current points and what plates each player has found.
  • You can also see a map of your trip with pins for every plate found. The pin will show you where the plate was found.
  • If you have created a Tour, start another game your next leg of your trip.
